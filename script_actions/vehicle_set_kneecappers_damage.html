vehicle_set_kneecappers_damage(vehicle_name, damage)
Parameters:
vehicle_name (string) name of the vehicle
damage (int) Amount of damage a kneecapper should inflict. 0 to apply damage equal to the tires hit points.
 
Returns:
none
Description:
Overrides the damage a kneecapper applies to a tire
