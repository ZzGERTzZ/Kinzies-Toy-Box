party_use_melee_weapons_only(melee_only)
Parameters:
melee_only (boolean) set to true to force the player and his followers to only use melee weapons, or false to allow use of any weapons
 
Returns:
none
Description:
Enable/disable use of non-melee weapons by the player and his followers.
