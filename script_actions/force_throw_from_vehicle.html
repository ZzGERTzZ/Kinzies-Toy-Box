force_throw_from_vehicle(name, throw_at_closest_human)
Parameters:
name (string) name of the the NPC
throw_at_closest_human (boolean) set to true to throw the weapon at the closest human, or false to throw at the closest player
 
Returns:
none
Description:
Force an NPC to toss a thrown weapon from a vehicle.
